# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, constructive collaboration.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Be considerate and professional in communication.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical outcomes.
- Give actionable feedback with clear context.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or hateful language.
- Doxxing, threats, or deliberate disruption.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or edit comments, issues, or pull requests that violate this policy and may restrict participation for repeated or severe violations.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it through a private channel available in the repository host (for example, direct maintainer contact or platform reporting tools). Include links and relevant context.
